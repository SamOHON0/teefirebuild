About Us

Two decades of fire safety expertise

Founded in 2004 by Timmy England, TEE Fire Safety Solutions has grown into one of Ireland's most trusted fire protection specialists, serving clients from airports to hospitals nationwide.

Our Story

From electrical contractor to Ireland's trusted fire safety partner.

20+

Years in Business

The Company

TEE (Timmy England Electrical) was started in 2004 by Timmy England, primarily as an electrical contracting business. Our main frame of works concentrated on the installation of fire alarm systems for all the major fire alarm companies operating in Ireland at the time.

Over the years that followed, TEE was involved in some of the largest system installations in the country, including at Kerry General Hospital, Naas General Hospital, Temple Street Hospital, Dublin Airport T1 and T2, and the ESB Money Point facility, which has the only fibre laser fire alarm and detection and deluge system in Europe. This system protects the movement of highly combustible coal from the ship to the stock yard, and subsequently from the stock yard to the interceptor.

The Expansion

In late 2008, we made the decision to expand our efforts and set up TEE Fire Safety Solutions, with the aim of providing a one-stop shop for the client. This enables us to carry out the supply, installation, commissioning and maintenance not only of fire alarm systems, but also of associated systems such as emergency lighting, suppression systems, moisture detection and fire extinguishers.

From our customers' perspective, this gives a very important comfort factor on any project, large or small. It means the client has one point of contact and all aspects are channelled through that contact.

Today

TEE Fire Safety Solutions has become a leading fire protection installation, commissioning and maintenance company in Ireland. We have continued to grow and develop to meet the needs of our clients, and our reputation for professionalism and quality service is unrivalled in the fire safety and fire protection industry.

This has ensured that TEE Fire Safety Solutions are the fire protection and safety installation contractor of choice. Our aim is to provide a top quality service to each customer with a competitive price, and in compliance with the required Fire Industry Regulations.

We are proud members of both the F.I.A.I. (Fire Industry Association of Ireland) and F.E.S.E. (Fire Engineering Systems Association).
