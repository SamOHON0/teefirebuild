Fire Alarm Systems

Full design, supply and installation of addressable and conventional fire alarm systems. From single-zone panels to large networked installations.

Commissioning

Independent commissioning of fire alarm and detection systems to IS 3218 and BS 5839. Full certification and documentation for every project.

Learn more

Maintenance & Servicing

Scheduled maintenance contracts to keep your systems compliant and operational. Quarterly, bi-annual and annual plans, nationwide.

Emergency Lighting
