Project

Kerry General Hospital

In 2013 TEE completed the installation of a new addressable fire alarm and an addressable emergency lighting system throughout Tralee General Hospital.

We worked with the staff in a live hospital to deliver a project on time and without any downtime or interference with the patients or staff. This is an acute hospital, and we worked closely with the on site contact and his team, including the infection control team and their measures.

Commissioning of this project was by G4S.
