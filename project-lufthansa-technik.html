The Relationship

Lufthansa Technik Airmotive Ireland operates a major aircraft engine maintenance facility in Dublin. TEE has been their electrical and fire safety partner for over eight years, delivering a range of works across the site including structured wiring, fire alarm upgrades, emergency lighting, and ad-hoc reactive maintenance.

This is the kind of long-term client relationship that matters most to us. When a site team can pick up the phone at short notice and know the right response is coming, that is the standard we build our business around.

What We Deliver
